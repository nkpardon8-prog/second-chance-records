# Research: Second Chance Records (Portland, OR)

## Research Question
Who is Tasha Brain, what is Second Chance Records, and what do we need to know to build their website?

---

## Executive Summary

Second Chance Records is an independent vinyl record store in Portland's Mt. Tabor neighborhood, opened in **July 2025** by **Tasha Brain**. The store specializes in **restored used vinyl records and affordable band t-shirts**, with a deep community mission centered on second chances — both for damaged records and for people reentering society after incarceration. The store's logo is an **open cell door**, reflecting this dual mission. The shop has earned local press coverage from Willamette Week and KMHD Jazz Radio, and has a growing social media presence. There is no existing website — the Phase 1 build will be the store's first web presence beyond social media and Yelp.

### 2. About Tasha Brain

Tasha Brain is the sole owner and operator of Second Chance Records. Key details:

- She personally **curates every item** in the store's inventory
- Each record goes through a meticulous restoration process:
  1. Cleaned with a **Spin-Clean manual washing system**
  2. Further cleaned with a **HumminGuru ultrasonic cleaner**
  3. Each disc is **listened to** personally
  4. Placed in a **fresh inner sleeve**
  5. **Condition notes** marked on the price sticker
- She is described as having "joyful, careful enthusiasm" for her work ([KMHD](https://www.kmhd.org/article/2026/01/05/record-store-rundown/))
- Strong advocate for criminal justice reform and reentry support

#### Tasha's Personal Story & Advocacy

Tasha has **lived experience** with the criminal justice system. She celebrated **15 years of freedom since her parole date on May 19, 2010**, when she was released to Bend, Oregon. This lived experience is the driving force behind the store's mission and name.

**Her advocacy focuses on:**
- **Prison reform** — systemic changes to the justice system
- **Successful reentry programs** — supporting people transitioning out of incarceration
- **Second chance employment** — fair hiring for justice-involved individuals
- **Automatic expungement** — removing barriers for those who've served their time
- **Community outreach** — she has spread hope, employment opportunities, and resources inside **Eastern Oregon Correctional Institution** and **Two Rivers Correctional Institution**

Her LinkedIn headline reads: **"Second Chances for hi-fi AND humans!"** — perfectly capturing the dual mission.

She uses the platform of selling used vinyl records to advocate for individuals who — like the records — have been given another opportunity to be heard. The focus is on restoring **dignity, purpose, and opportunity** to people impacted by the justice system.

**Source:** [LinkedIn — Tasha Brain](https://www.linkedin.com/in/tasha-brain/)

**Quote from Tasha Brain:**
> "I'm a huge advocate of second chances for people. I've really started to look more at this problem with people who don't get a second chance when they reenter society after prison. The barriers they come across are just unbelievable."
— [Willamette Week, Sept 2025](https://www.wweek.com/music/2025/09/09/four-portland-record-stores-to-keep-in-heavy-rotation/)

### 9. Building & Physical Location

The building at 5744 E Burnside St is a **1946-era flex/commercial building** (~3,150 SF total, including ~2,100 SF main + storage shop) on a 0.25-acre lot. Zoned CG (Commercial General). Class C building with multiple tenants. Transit score of 64.

Other tenants in the building include **Coplin Architecture** and **Artisan Woodworking & Design** — giving the space an artisan/creative feel that aligns well with SCR's brand.

**For the "Visit" page illustration:** Client wants a custom drawing of the building + "how to find me" wayfinding. This will need to be created — no existing illustration found. The building is a modest commercial structure; a hand-drawn or illustrated style would match the store's warm, approachable brand.

**Source:** [LoopNet](https://www.loopnet.com/Listing/18770604/5744-East-Burnside-Portland-OR/), [CityFeet](https://www.cityfeet.com/cont/listing/5744-e-burnside-st-portland-or-97215/cs12959685)
